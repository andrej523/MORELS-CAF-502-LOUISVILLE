Morels Café 502 Louisville

A Deli, But Also a Café

Morels Cafe is Louisville's finest destination for vegan food that doesn't involve bland greens, eggplant, or tofu. We like to indulge in the finer foods, like Philly Cheesesteak Fries, Buffalo Wings, and of course, Farbys. We also offer amazing foods to be taken home in our deli, as well as a full line of Kentucky Kombucha on tap.
